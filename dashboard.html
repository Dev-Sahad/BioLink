Add this record to your DNS provider (Cloudflare, Namecheap, GoDaddy, etc.):

How It Works
  • 1️⃣ Enter your domain or subdomain above
  • 2️⃣ Add the CNAME record shown to your DNS
  • 3️⃣ Wait for DNS propagation (up to 48h)
  • 4️⃣ Click "Mark as Verified" — your URL updates instantly
