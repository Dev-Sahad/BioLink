What's Inside v2.0
🔗
Unlimited Links
Add as many links as you want with custom icons, descriptions, and tags (New, Free, Hot…).
🎵
Music Embed
Spotify, SoundCloud, or custom MP3 player with playback controls right on your bio page.
📺
Video Embed
YouTube, Vimeo, or direct MP4 video embedded beautifully with a 16:9 aspect ratio player.
🖼️
Photo Gallery
3-column gallery grid with lightbox viewer — tap any photo to open it full-screen.
🎨
8 Themes
Dark, Light, Cyber, Midnight, Forest, Rose, Vapor, Sand — plus custom accent color picker.
✨
Visual Effects
Floating particles, cursor trail, snowfall, and spinning avatar ring to stand out.
🌐
Custom Domain
Point your own domain to your BioLink with step-by-step DNS setup instructions.
📱
Mobile-First UI
Bottom nav, mobile status bar simulation, and touch-optimized interactions throughout.
📊
Analytics
Track page views and link clicks per user. Admin sees platform-wide stats at a glance.
🔐
Auth System
Full register/login/logout flow with role-based access (user vs. admin) and account suspension.
⚙️
Admin Dashboard
Create, edit, suspend, or delete any user. Manage all bio pages. Export all data as JSON.
🤖
QR Code
Every bio page has a floating QR code button for instant offline sharing — no library needed.
⌨️
Typewriter Effect
Animated typewriter for your tagline. Separate multiple roles with · and they cycle automatically.
🎖️
Profile Badges
Add custom emoji badges to your profile — Verified, Creator, Premium, and anything you want.
🚀
GitHub Pages Ready
Zero backend required. Deploy in 5 minutes — upload 7 files, enable Pages, done.
